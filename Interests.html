My Car

  1. For my 18th birthday, my dad and I bought a 1957 Standard 8. It was in poor condition.
    Over the past few months, we've been fixing it up. Here it is on day 1.
  2. We transported it on a trailer to my Grandad's garage, where we set to work stripping its interior.
    The outside was very rusted. There were holes in the body. The carpet was moldy. The chairs were in good nick though!
    We started sanding it down then.
  3. Some parts were painted and being fitted while some still looked terrible. So much welding had to be done.
    In the third photo, the front fenders and bumper is primered, but the bonnet is still in poor condition!
  4. With our pieces welded and fixed, I personally had paint chosen and mixed. It had a glossy sheen.
    I also chose to paint it in two tone, contrasting the single peach colour it came in.
  5. With some of its pieces put back on, this is the current state. There's still interior work to be done.
    The engine is running, there's just no exhaust or fuel line! But we're working on it.
    Parts are so so hard to come by, and some it is jury-rigged, but we love our car a lot.

History

I have a love for history. I have always loved the topic and I thoroughly enjoy learning about the past.
I've done the family history by using our National Archives. I did it over the course of a week.
I had to apply to use the documents, and they weren't always complete. It took a lot of investigation and thorough searching.
In the end, I had marriage certs, birth certs and some death certs for many family members extending back to the 1800s!

I also collect a lot of old artefacts and antique things. I have several collections I'm very proud of.
I have a very large old stamp collection. These stamps come from literally all over the world, even from some places that no longer exist.
My most treasured one is Grace Kelly's marriage stamps. They were issued in 1950 to celeberate her marriage to Prince Rainier.
I own two typewriters. I own a large coin collection. I own a 20s French Rapier. I have several pieces of ww1 and ww2 military equipment.
I collect a lot of old books, rings, statues and other assorted oddities.These are all really important to me, and my collection is forever growing!
