If I Never Sleep

If I never sleep
I'll never see your face.
It hurts me too much when I do.
And you only appear when I sleep anyway
Since the heavens took you away.

Isolationism

Isolationism
Is my only refuge.

When I know I am home
But I still feel lost.

When my friends call my name
But I feel alone.

when my corner of the world is invaded
And my closely guarded feelings spill.

When the things that I wish to protect are hurt
And I can only look on in vain.

No one understands my pain.
So comes my lonely deluge.

Isolationism

Is my only refuge.

Americano, My Cat

But most every night.

You live now inside of me
along with all the rest.
You'll always be the midnight visitor
In the heart within my chest.

To One Lost

My aching bones all marrowed with regret
Hold up an ancient veil, distant eyes wet.
Single sorrow frown, lying in black ground
In a lonely land, nightingales surround.

And bless us with a melody, so sweet and clear, the sound!
Please fret not thee, lamenting bird, for I am all around.

Whispers

The mighty oak holds up a leafy crown
By river's edge, an ancient shadow walks
And listens to whispering reeds in time,
A hushed symphony of times long gone by.

A rotten dam of fate blocks the path
And collapses under its own dark weight.
Then it scatters memories all around
Picked up by the grass, and told to the ground.

The ground told the sea, the sea told the air
The lone shadow begged, how could that be fair?
Then shadow sat down, shrouded by the weeds
And told us old lies, so he could be free

Why do we speak of others so blindly?
If you somehow know, I beg you, come find me.

Depth

The Depth of Water
is all relative to the depth of that
which the water is contained in.

The depth of my love
extends deeper than any other thing.
It does not compare

to the measurement of the happiness
and love deep within my heart
when I hold your hand in mine.

When you lay your head on my shoulder,
and we fall asleep beneath the unending display
of light and wonder above.

I will forever be
entranced by your smile
and the inch of sadness

That I feel when we part each night.
The depth of my love is uncomparible
to the universe.

Nanny

The world has changed since you left
Or perhaps I've just grown up, and
see the cracks in the ice of society.
The poverty, the killings, inequality and theft.
And people try so hard to deal with it in
their own ways - hard work, suicide, insobriety.

I still hold to my heart the thought that if you were still here
The world but not be such a dark place,
that maybe you could have shielded us grandkids.
However, I find myself now writing in this book,
my hand trembling with fear
And wishing that I could lie in bed for eternity
and to never again feel the pain of opening my eyelids.

I miss you, more than I can articulate.
and I hope that you're happy and so content, wherever you are.
I'll ensure my kids know the hero you were
Your tale, I'll reiterate.
And tell them that if they need you, you're always here
Look up, find the most beautiful and brightest star.
